Altered Tracker

This tool is meant to track counts for Altered TCG by Equinox. It can also replace the need to have the adventure cards laid out on the table, or can be used to inform the layout of the physical cards

For best results, it is recommended to "install" this tool as an application on your phone.

  • For iOS, use the "Share" button and select "Add to Home Screen".
  • For Android, tap the menu icon (three dots in the top right) and choose "Add to Home Screen"

Usage

Click the "Start" button below to begin. Use the right and left sides of the colored stat sections to increase and decrease the value for that stat, respectively.

In Auto Mode (the default) you can select if an expedition has a Defender by pressing the "H Def" and "C Def" buttons, and you can auto advance the expeditions based on the target stats by pressing the "Advance" button.

In Manual Mode (press the "Manual Mode" button at the bottom of your control section to switch) you can control each expedition individually with the H+ and C+ buttons to advance and H- and C- to back them up. This can also be used for handling cards like Sakarabru.
